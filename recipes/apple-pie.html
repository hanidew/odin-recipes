Apple Pie

This classic apple pie recipe is made with a buttery, flaky crust and a sweet, cinnamon-spiced apple filling. Perfect for any occasion!

Apple pie is a traditional dessert that has been enjoyed for generations.

Ingredients:

  • 16 small Granny Smith apples
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Peel and core apples, then thinly slice. Set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and stir to form a paste; cook until fragrant, about 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. RPress one pastry into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie pan.
  4. Place sliced apples into the bottom crust, forming a slight mound.
