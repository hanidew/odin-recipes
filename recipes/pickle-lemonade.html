Pickle Lemonade

Nutrition Facts: Contains 124 Calories

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup freshly-squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup dill pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Add lemon juice, pickle juice, and honey to a small pitcher or jar, and stir until well combined.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes, and pour in pickle-lemon mixture. Top with lemon seltzer, and give it a stir.
  4. Garnish with lemon slices and a pickle spear, and enjoy.
